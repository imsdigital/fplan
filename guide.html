User's Guide for fplan

John C. Peterson, <jaypee@netcom.com>

Release 1.4.2, 01 May 1999

This is the user's guide for fplan, a freely distributable application designed to assist general aviation pilots in creating flight plans for cross country flights in powered aircraft. This document contains information on the installation of the software, the creation of planfiles that describe the proposed flight, and the interpretation of fplan output.
